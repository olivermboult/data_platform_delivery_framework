# Knowledge Transfer & Capability

## Establish the Internal Team Structure and Capability

**Activities**
- Document the size and structure of the internal data team, including roles and areas of responsibility.
- Capture the technical background and experience of the internal data team, including familiarity with tools, languages, and platforms currently in use.
- Record whether team members are employed on a permanent or contracted basis, and identify any implications for continuity and knowledge retention.
- Identify any known resourcing constraints that may affect the project, such as limited internal availability or planned organisational changes.
- Identify key person dependencies within the customer's team and document the risks these present to project continuity.
- Identify any gaps in current team roles or headcount that could affect the customer's ability to operate a data capability independently after go-live.
- Identify who is expected to take responsibility for ongoing administration and maintenance of the delivered solution, and capture their current technical skills and experience.
- Capture whether the customer intends to build internal capability during the project or rely on ongoing external support beyond go-live.

**Outcomes**
- Documented size and structure of the internal data team, including roles and areas of responsibility.
- Documented technical background and experience of the internal data team, including familiarity with tools, languages, and platforms in use.
- Documented employment basis of team members and any implications for continuity and knowledge retention.
- Documented resourcing constraints that may affect the project.
- Documented key person dependencies and associated risks to project continuity.
- Documented gaps in team roles or headcount that could affect independent operation after go-live.
- Identified individuals responsible for ongoing administration and maintenance, including their current technical capability.
- Documented whether the customer intends to build internal capability or rely on ongoing external support beyond go-live.

**Pre-requisites**
- Named HR or team leadership contacts with knowledge of the internal team structure are available for a workshop.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Size of the internal data team
- Complexity of team structure and role definitions
- Extent of resourcing constraints or key person dependencies

## Capture Training and Knowledge Transfer Requirements

**Activities**
- Capture the customer's expectations around training and knowledge transfer, including whether these are expected as formal deliverables.
- Identify specific areas where training or upskilling will be required, covering both the data team and business end users.
- Capture the current level of data literacy across the wider business and identify any areas where additional support may be needed to support effective use of data and reporting outputs.
- Capture the tools, access, and skills currently available to support self-service capability, and capture whether self-service is expected as a project outcome.
- Identify any existing training materials, documentation, or onboarding processes that could be relevant to the project.
- Capture any constraints on training delivery, including preferred formats, scheduling restrictions, and team availability.
- Capture the customer's expectations around project documentation, including preferred formats, ownership, and the standards for maintaining materials after go-live.

**Outcomes**
- Documented customer expectations for training and knowledge transfer, including whether these are expected as formal deliverables.
- Identified areas where training or upskilling will be required, covering data team and business end users.
- Documented data literacy levels across the wider business and any areas requiring additional support.
- Documented tools, access, and skills available for self-service capability, and whether self-service is expected as a project outcome.
- Identified existing training materials, documentation, or onboarding processes relevant to the project.
- Documented constraints on training delivery, including preferred formats and scheduling restrictions.
- Documented customer expectations for project documentation, including preferred formats, ownership, and post-go-live maintenance standards.

**Pre-requisites**
- Outputs from the Establish the Internal Team Structure and Capability task are available.
- Named stakeholders from business operations and relevant team leadership are available for a workshop.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Size and data literacy level of the business user population
- Number of distinct user groups requiring training
- Extent of existing training materials available
- Complexity of documentation requirements

# Design - Finance

## Budget Confirmation

- Confirm the total approved budget for the Delivery stage and verify the full scope of the proposed design remains within it.
- Confirm that the proposed design for each confirmed use case meets the success criteria and KPIs agreed during Discovery, and document any deviations with a clear rationale.
- Document any cost implications arising from design decisions made during Design, and confirm these have been communicated to and accepted by the Project Sponsor before the Delivery Plan is issued.
- Document any descoped items, deferred workloads, or cost assumptions that affect the budget or timeline of delivery, and confirm these have been accepted by the Project Sponsor.
- Confirm that budget exists for any future phases identified during Discovery and that these are not inadvertently drawn into the current scope.

## Commercial Structure

- Confirm the rates applicable to each role and produce a full cost breakdown across the Delivery stage, aligned to the phased delivery roadmap.
- Confirm the invoicing schedule and payment structure for the Delivery stage, and ensure this is consistent with the commercial arrangements agreed during Discovery.
- Confirm the commercial process for raising and approving changes to resource or scope during delivery where cost may be affected, and ensure this is documented in the Delivery Plan.
- Confirm any procurement or legal processes that must be completed before spend can be committed, and ensure these are resolved before the Delivery Plan is issued.

## Risks and Assumptions

- Document any financial risks and cost assumptions that carry uncertainty into the Delivery stage, and confirm the agreed approach for managing these.
- Confirm that budget authority has been established for the Delivery stage, identifying who holds sign-off authority for different categories of financial decision.

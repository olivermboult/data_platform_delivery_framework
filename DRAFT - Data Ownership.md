# Design - Data Ownership

## Ownership and Responsibilities

- Confirm the named Data Owner and Data Steward for each domain in scope, resolving any ownership gaps identified during Discovery before build begins.
- Document the data stewardship responsibilities and escalation paths for each domain, confirming who holds accountability at each step and how issues are raised and resolved.
- Document data lifecycle ownership from ingestion through to consumption, confirming who is responsible at each stage and how handoffs between stages are managed.
- Confirm how any changes to data ownership resulting from the project, such as new domains introduced or existing responsibilities restructured, will be reflected in the governance design.
- Confirm that all Data Owners and Data Stewards have been formally notified of their responsibilities and have agreed to them before build begins.

## Platform Governance

- Confirm how data ownership will be enforced within the platform, whether through workspace permissions, domain assignments, or documented policies, and ensure this is consistent with the access design confirmed during Platform Design.
- Confirm how regulatory and compliance obligations for relevant data domains are reflected in the ownership design, and document who holds accountability for meeting those obligations within each domain.
- Define the process for assigning ownership to new data domains or entities introduced after the initial delivery, to ensure the governance framework remains current as the solution evolves.

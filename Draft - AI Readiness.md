# AI Readiness

- Relates to the suitability of the customer's data and processes for the use of AI and machine learning workloads.
- Covers whether the data available meets the minimum requirements for the intended modelling approach in terms of volume, completeness, and consistency, whether the chosen platform and infrastructure can support the intended AI workloads, and whether any process changes are required alongside the technical delivery. Also covers the customer's internal capability to support and maintain AI workloads after delivery and any skills gaps that need to be addressed.
- Related subjects are Data Sources & Ingestion (Data & AI), which covers the availability and accessibility of data required for AI workloads, Data Quality (Data & AI), which covers the quality of data available for AI model training and validation, Technology Platform (Platform & Security), which covers the platform capability required to run AI workloads, Platform Performance (Platform & Security), which covers the infrastructure capacity required to support AI processing, and Knowledge Transfer & Capability (Business & Operations), which covers the customer's internal capability to operate and maintain AI workloads after delivery.

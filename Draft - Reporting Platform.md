# Reporting Platform

- Relates to the software and platforms used for reporting across the business.
- Covers the identification or confirmation of the reporting tool to be used in the new solution, any existing tools currently in place and whether they will be replaced or retained, licensing arrangements, known limitations or pain points with existing tooling, and the compatibility of the chosen reporting tool with the data platform direction confirmed during the project.
- Related subjects are Technology Platform (Platform & Security), which covers the data platform that the reporting tool must integrate with, and Report Migration (Reporting & Analysis), which covers the assessment and recreation of existing reports where the reporting tool is being replaced.

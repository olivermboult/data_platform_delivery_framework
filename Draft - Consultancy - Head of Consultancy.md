# Head of Consultancy

The Head of Consultancy is accountable for the commercial standards of all delivery engagements. This role is not involved in the day-to-day running of projects but engages at defined points across the Definition stage and wherever risks, decisions, or escalations require senior involvement beyond the Data Lead.

**Framework Involvement**
- Reviews proposals before they are issued to customers.
- Provides internal sign-off on the Initial Recommendation at the close of Pre-Discovery before any further engagement begins.
- Provides internal sign-off on all Discovery outputs at the close of Discovery before progression to Design.
- Provides internal sign-off on all Design outputs at the close of Design before the Delivery stage begins.
- Acts as the escalation point for risks, issues, or decisions that cannot be resolved by the Data Lead during Discovery.
- Consults with the Head of TechOps at the close of Live to confirm whether findings or new use cases identified during that phase should be returned to Pre-Discovery before any further delivery begins.

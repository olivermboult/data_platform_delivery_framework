# Head of TechOps

The Head of TechOps is accountable for the ongoing operation and support of live solutions. This role is not involved in the day-to-day running of projects but engages at defined points during the Live phase and wherever risks, decisions, or escalations require senior involvement beyond the Data Lead.

**Framework Involvement**

- Oversees the support function on an ongoing basis throughout Live, maintaining accountability for service quality and operational continuity.
- Acts as the escalation point for risks, issues, or decisions that cannot be resolved by the Data Lead during Live.
- Consults with the Head of Consultancy to confirm whether findings, enhancements, or new use cases identified during Live should be returned to Pre-Discovery before any further delivery begins.

# Data Consultant

The Data Consultant is the primary delivery role within the Definition stage, responsible for conducting discovery sessions and producing the outputs that underpin the Pre-Discovery, Discovery, and Design phases. All outputs produced by the Data Consultant are reviewed and collated by the Data Lead before progressing to internal sign-off. On larger projects, multiple Data Consultants may be assigned, with the Data Lead coordinating across them. The Data Consultant role encompasses all data-related specialisms, including data architecture, data governance, and data strategy. More senior Data Consultants will typically lead a broader range of sessions and take ownership of more complex outputs, while less experienced consultants may focus on specific subjects under the oversight of the Data Lead.

**Framework Involvement**

Pre-Discovery
- Supports Business Initiation, Platform Data and AI Initiation, and Reporting Initiation sessions, capturing detail and contributing subject matter expertise across data, governance, and architecture subjects.
- Contributes to the production of the Pre-Discovery Summary and Risk and Feasibility Note under the direction of the Data Lead.

Discovery
- Delivers Use Case Discovery, Business-Data Alignment, Operating Model Discovery, Platform Discovery, Source System Discovery, and AI Discovery sessions, producing initial outputs for review by the Data Lead.
- Applies data architecture, data governance, and data strategy expertise across relevant subjects, including data modelling, data ownership, and operating model design.
- Contributes to the production of the discovery report under the direction of the Data Lead.

Design 
- Delivers Design sessions across data and governance subjects, producing initial outputs for review by the Data Lead.
- Leads the production of data-related Design outputs, including data layer design, source to target mappings, data lineage documentation, and data quality framework design.
- Applies data architecture expertise to confirm that the Design accurately reflects the agreed requirements before outputs are submitted for stakeholder review.
- Contributes to estimate and project plan production under the direction of the Data Lead.

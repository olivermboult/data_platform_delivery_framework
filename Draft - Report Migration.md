# Report Migration

- Relates to the recreation, retirement, or rationalisation of existing reports and their underlying logic from a legacy reporting tool within the new solution.
- Covers the assessment of the existing report estate, the translation of report logic and calculations into the new environment, cutover planning, the involvement of existing report owners in validating recreated reports, and the decommissioning of legacy reports once migration is complete.
- Related subjects are Reporting Platform (Reporting & Analysis), which covers the legacy and new reporting tools involved in the migration, and Report Design (Reporting & Analysis), which covers the design process that recreated reports are validated against.

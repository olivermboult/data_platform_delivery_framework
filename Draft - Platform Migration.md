# Platform Migration

- Relates to the decommissioning or transition away from an existing data platform or tooling stack.
- Covers the assessment of the legacy platform, the sequencing of workloads being moved to the new environment, parallel running arrangements where the legacy and new platforms operate alongside each other during transition, rollback planning, and the controlled retirement of legacy infrastructure once migration is complete.
- Related subjects are Technology Platform (Platform & Security), which covers the new platform the solution is being migrated to, and Development & Deployment (Platform & Security), which covers how the new environment is structured and how workloads are promoted through it.

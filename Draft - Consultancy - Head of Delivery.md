# Head of Delivery

The Head of Delivery is accountable for the quality standards of all delivery engagements. This role is not involved in the day-to-day running of projects but engages at defined points across the Delivery and Operation stages and wherever risks, decisions, or escalations require senior involvement beyond the Data Lead.

**Framework Involvement**

- Reviews the Platform, Build, and Reporting completion notes produced by the Data Lead at the close of the Delivery stage and provides internal sign-off on the full Delivery stage before Quality Assurance begins.
- Reviews the Data Lead's assessment that Quality Assurance is complete and the solution is ready for release, and provides internal sign-off before the Release phase begins.
- Reviews the Data Lead's assessment that Release is complete and the solution is ready to pass to the support function, and provides internal sign-off before Live begins.
- Acts as the escalation point for risks, issues, or decisions that cannot be resolved by the Data Lead during the Delivery stage, Quality Assurance, and Release.

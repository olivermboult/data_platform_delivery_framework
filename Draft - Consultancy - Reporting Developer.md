# Reporting Developer

The Reporting Developer is the primary delivery role during the Reporting phase, responsible for building all reports and dashboards in line with the agreed wireframes and Design. This role contributes reporting expertise during the Definition stage and Design, and supports the Quality Assurance and Release phases by resolving reporting issues as they arise. On larger projects, multiple Reporting Developers may be assigned, with the Data Lead coordinating across them. The Reporting Developer role encompasses seniority levels from Reporting Developer through to Senior Reporting Developer. More senior developers will typically take ownership of more complex reports and broader areas of the reporting layer, while less experienced developers may focus on specific reports or components under the oversight of the Data Lead.

**Framework Involvement**

Discovery
- Contributes to Business-Data Alignment sessions led by the Data Consultant, providing reporting expertise to inform storyboarding and wireframe design decisions.
- Contributes reporting expertise across reporting subjects during Platform Discovery and Source System Discovery where relevant.

Design 
- Leads the production of reporting wireframes for all priority dashboards and reports, working under the oversight of the Data Consultant.
- Contributes technical input across reporting subjects, including report usage, report creation, and reporting performance.
- Contributes to the validation of wireframes with business stakeholders before build begins.

Platform
- Advises on reporting tool configuration during the Platform phase, ensuring the reporting environment is set up in a way that supports the agreed reporting design before build begins.

Build
- Contributes to semantic layer design during the Build phase, ensuring the semantic layer is structured in a way that supports the agreed reporting requirements before the Reporting phase begins.

Reporting 
- Leads the build of all reports and dashboards confirmed in the approved wireframes.
- Validates report outputs against source data and the agreed business logic throughout the build.
- Applies row-level security and access controls across all reports and user groups in line with the agreed access design.
- Configures distribution to all agreed user groups via the agreed methods, ready to be enabled at release.
- Conducts peer reviews of all reports, documenting outcomes and resolving any issues identified before the phase closes.
- Produces the reporting completion note for review and sign-off by the Data Lead.

Quality Assurance
- Resolves defects identified in the reporting layer during testing, documenting outcomes and confirming resolution before the phase closes.

Release
- Validates that report outputs meet expectations in the production environment immediately following release and resolves any reporting issues identified during the initial post-release period.

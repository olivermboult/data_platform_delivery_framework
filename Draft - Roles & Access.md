# Roles & Access

- Relates to who can see and interact with data and platform resources, and under what conditions.
- Covers the definition of user personas and their permissions across the platform, row-level and object-level security requirements, guest and external user access, service principal and automated access for pipelines and deployments, and the processes for requesting, approving, and reviewing access over time.
- Related subjects are Data Protection (Platform & Security), which covers the sensitivity classifications that inform access restrictions, and Data Ownership (Data & AI), which covers the accountability structures that determine who holds authority over specific data assets.

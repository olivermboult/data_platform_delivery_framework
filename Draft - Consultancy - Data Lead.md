# Data Lead

The Data Lead is responsible for the end-to-end management and delivery of the project from Pre-Discovery through to the close of Release. This role holds accountability for all Definition outputs, the day-to-day running of the project across all Delivery phases, and the active management of Quality Assurance and Release. The Data Lead is the primary point of contact for the customer's project sponsor throughout the engagement and works closely with the Head of Consultancy and Head of Delivery to ensure the project is correctly resourced and commercially managed.

**Framework Involvement**

Definition
- Authors proposals for review by the Head of Consultancy before they are issued to customers.
- Works with the Head of Consultancy and Head of Delivery to resource the project, confirming the team composition required for each phase.
- Holds responsibility for all outputs across Pre-Discovery, Discovery, and Design, authoring those the Data Lead leads directly and reviewing and collating those produced by other roles before submitting for Head of Consultancy sign-off.
- Is capable of leading all Discovery sessions directly but may delegate specific sessions to the appropriate specialist role, including Business Analysts for reporting initiation and use case discovery, Data Consultants for data sessions, and AI/ML Engineers for AI sessions.
- Leads finance and governance conversations directly throughout Definition.
- Provides a clear assessment and recommendation to the Head of Consultancy at the close of Pre-Discovery, Discovery, and Design to support internal sign-off.
- Acts as the escalation point for the customer's project sponsor during Discovery, engaging the Head of Consultancy where risks or decisions exceed their own authority.
- Manages commercial aspects of the project throughout Discovery, including tracking costs against budget and managing change requests where scope or cost may be affected.

Delivery
- Is actively involved in the day-to-day running and delivery of the project across the Platform, Build, and Reporting phases.
- Works with the Head of Consultancy and Head of Delivery to confirm team composition at the start of each Delivery phase.
- Holds responsibility for ensuring entry and exit criteria are met at each phase transition throughout Delivery.
- Provides internal sign-off at the close of the Platform, Build, and Reporting phases, producing a completion note and clear assessment for review by the Head of Delivery.
- Manages commercial aspects of the project throughout Delivery, including tracking costs against budget and managing change requests where scope or cost may be affected.
- Acts as the primary point of contact for the customer's project sponsor throughout Delivery.

Quality Assurance and Release
- Actively runs the Quality Assurance and Release phases, holding responsibility for all outputs and phase management.
- Provides a clear assessment and recommendation to the Head of Delivery at the close of Quality Assurance confirming the solution is ready for release.
- Provides a clear assessment and recommendation to the Head of Delivery at the close of Release confirming the solution is ready to pass to the support function.
- Acts as the primary point of contact for the customer's project sponsor through to the close of Release.

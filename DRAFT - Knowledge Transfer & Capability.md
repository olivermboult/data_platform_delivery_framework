# Design - Skills & Capabilities

## Training and Enablement

- Define the training plan based on the skill gaps and training requirements identified during Discovery, confirming the topics to be covered, the format and timing of sessions, and the participants required from the customer's organisation.
- Confirm that self-service capability, where agreed as an outcome during Discovery, is addressed within the training plan and that the relevant users have been identified and made available.
- Confirm the training plan has been reviewed and agreed with the customer and that the necessary participants have confirmed their availability before build begins.
- Confirm whether any ongoing consultancy or managed service arrangement is expected beyond the initial delivery, and ensure this is reflected in the project scope and commercial terms.

## Handover and Documentation

- Define the knowledge transfer and handover approach, including the documentation to be produced such as user guides and administrator guides, the sessions to be delivered, and the sign-off criteria that must be met before responsibility transfers to the customer.
- Identify any roles or responsibilities within the operating model that remain unfilled and confirm the customer's plan to address these before go-live.

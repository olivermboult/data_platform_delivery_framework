# Audit & Compliance

## Identify Regulatory and Compliance Obligations

**Activities**
- Identify the regulatory and compliance frameworks that apply to the project, covering any industry-specific regulations, data handling obligations, and internal standards.
- Identify whether data residency requirements apply, including any restrictions on where data can be stored or processed geographically.
- Identify any known compliance gaps in the current technical environment that the project will need to address or mitigate.
- Identify whether any regulatory actions, notices, or outstanding remediation obligations are currently in progress within the organisation.

**Outcomes**
- Documented regulatory and compliance frameworks applicable to the project, including industry-specific regulations, data handling obligations, and internal standards.
- Documented data residency requirements and any restrictions on where data can be stored or processed.
- Documented known compliance gaps in the current technical environment, identified as risks or obligations for the project.
- Documented regulatory actions, notices, or outstanding remediation obligations currently in progress within the organisation.

**Pre-requisites**
- A stakeholder with responsibility for compliance or regulatory affairs is available for a workshop.
- Any existing compliance documentation or audit reports are accessible prior to the workshop where available.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Number of regulatory frameworks applicable to the project
- Complexity of data residency or cross-border requirements
- Extent of known compliance gaps or outstanding obligations

## Capture Audit Requirements

**Activities**
- Identify the audit trail requirements the project must meet, covering what events must be recorded and the required retention period for audit logs.
- Identify whether the customer is subject to internal compliance audits or external regulatory reviews, and capture what evidence will be required to support these.
- Identify whether any specific security standards apply to the project, such as ISO 27001 or Cyber Essentials, and capture the obligations these create.
- Identify whether existing systems currently produce audit logs and, if so, document their format, retention period, and how they can be accessed.

**Outcomes**
- Documented audit trail requirements, including events to be recorded and required retention periods.
- Documented internal and external audit obligations and the evidence required to support them.
- Documented security standards applicable to the project and the obligations they create.
- Documented existing audit log provision, including format, retention period, and access method.

**Pre-requisites**
- Outputs from the Identify Regulatory and Compliance Obligations task are available.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Number and complexity of audit trail requirements
- Number of applicable security standards
- Availability of existing audit log documentation

## Identify Compliance Governance and Responsibilities

**Activities**
- Identify who holds responsibility for compliance sign-off within the customer's organisation and document their involvement at key project stages.
- Identify whether there is a formal compliance review or approval process the project must pass through, and capture at which points this will be required.
- Identify any compliance obligations that apply to third-party platforms or cloud services in use or under consideration, and document who is responsible for meeting them.
- Identify whether any third parties or suppliers hold contractual rights to audit the customer's systems or data.

**Outcomes**
- Identified compliance sign-off authority within the customer's organisation and documented involvement at key project stages.
- Documented formal compliance review or approval process and the project stages at which it will be required.
- Documented compliance obligations applicable to third-party platforms or cloud services, and identified responsible parties.
- Documented any third-party or supplier contractual audit rights applicable to the customer's systems or data.

**Pre-requisites**
- Outputs from the Identify Regulatory and Compliance Obligations task are available.
- A named compliance or legal contact is available for a workshop.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Number of third-party platforms or services with compliance obligations
- Complexity of compliance review and approval processes
- Number of stakeholders involved in compliance governance

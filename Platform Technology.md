# Platform Technology

## Review the Existing Platform and Infrastructure

**Activities**
- Identify whether the customer has an existing data platform or whether this is a greenfield build.
- Document the customer's current cloud and infrastructure environment, including the cloud providers and hosting models in use.
- Identify whether the customer has an existing relationship, enterprise agreement, or active licensing with any data platform provider.
- Identify any existing investments in tooling, training, or infrastructure that are relevant to the choice of data platform.

**Outcomes**
- Documented whether the customer has an existing data platform or whether this is a greenfield build.
- Documented current cloud and infrastructure environment, including cloud providers and hosting models in use.
- Documented existing relationships, enterprise agreements, or active licensing with data platform providers.
- Documented existing investments in tooling, training, or infrastructure relevant to platform selection.

**Pre-requisites**
- A technical contact with knowledge of the existing platform and infrastructure is available for a workshop.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Complexity of the existing cloud and infrastructure environment
- Number of existing platform relationships or agreements to review

## Establish the Platform Selection Position

**Activities**
- Identify whether the platform choice has already been made, and if so document the platform, its structural approach, and the basis for the decision.
- Identify whether the platform selection is open to recommendation, and if so capture the customer's key requirements, preferences, and priorities to inform the evaluation.
- Identify any internal IT or procurement policies that restrict or influence which platform providers can be used.

**Outcomes**
- Documented platform selection position, including the platform chosen and basis for the decision where a choice has been made.
- Documented customer requirements, preferences, and priorities to inform platform evaluation where selection remains open.
- Documented IT or procurement policies that restrict or influence platform provider options.

**Pre-requisites**
- Outputs from the Review the Existing Platform and Infrastructure task are available.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Whether the platform has been selected or an evaluation is required
- Number of procurement or IT policies to review

## Identify Platform Constraints and Compatibility Requirements

**Activities**
- Identify any constraints or limitations associated with the platforms under consideration, such as regional availability, feature gaps, or service quotas.
- Identify any data residency or sovereignty requirements that restrict which platforms or geographic regions can be used.
- Capture whether the customer has any concerns about vendor dependency or lock-in, and document the nature of those concerns.
- Identify any dependencies between the platform and other tools or systems in the customer's environment.

**Outcomes**
- Documented constraints or limitations associated with platforms under consideration, including regional availability, feature gaps, and service quotas.
- Documented data residency or sovereignty requirements restricting platform or regional options.
- Documented vendor dependency or lock-in concerns and their nature.
- Documented dependencies between the platform and other tools or systems in the customer's environment.

**Pre-requisites**
- Outputs from the Establish the Platform Selection Position task are available.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Number of platforms under consideration
- Complexity of data residency or sovereignty requirements
- Number of dependencies between the platform and other systems

---

## Capture Platform Commercial and Support Requirements

**Activities**
- Capture the licensing and cost model associated with the platform, and identify any cost implications for the project or for ongoing operations.
- Capture whether a vendor support contract or managed service arrangement is already in place or will be required as part of the solution.
- Capture whether platform procurement is subject to any approval processes or timelines that could affect the project.

**Outcomes**
- Documented platform licensing and cost model, including cost implications for the project and ongoing operations.
- Documented vendor support contract or managed service arrangements in place or required.
- Documented platform procurement approval processes and any timelines that could affect the project.

**Pre-requisites**
- Outputs from the Establish the Platform Selection Position task are available.

**Basis of Estimate**
- Complexity of platform licensing and cost model
- Whether procurement approval processes are required

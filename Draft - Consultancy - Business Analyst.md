# Business Analyst

The Business Analyst is assigned to projects where the scope or complexity warrants additional business-facing capacity, typically where there are a large number of use cases or a complex stakeholder environment. This role delivers non-technical discovery sessions and produces initial outputs for review by the Data Lead, working alongside the Data Consultant throughout the Definition stage. The Business Analyst has no involvement in the Delivery stage, Quality Assurance, or Release.

**Framework Involvement**

Pre-Discovery
- Delivers Reporting Initiation sessions where assigned, capturing detail on the customer's current reporting position, tooling, and reporting requirements.
- Produces initial outputs from Reporting Initiation sessions for review by the Data Lead.
- Supports proposal production under the direction of the Data Lead.

Discovery
- Leads Use Case Discovery sessions, working directly with the customer's stakeholders and subject matter experts to define and prioritise business use cases.
- Produces initial outputs from Use Case Discovery sessions, including use case definitions, pain points, success criteria, and prioritisation recommendations, for review by the Data Lead.
- Supports Business-Data Alignment sessions led by the Data Consultant, contributing business context and requirements knowledge to inform data model and reporting design decisions.
- Contributes to the production of the discovery report under the direction of the Data Lead.

Design
- Supports the production of non-technical Design outputs under the direction of the Data Lead where assigned.

# Operational Support

- Relates to how the solution is maintained and supported once it is live in production.
- Covers the responsibilities and processes that ensure issues are identified and resolved, including incident management, bug fixes, performance monitoring, and escalation paths. Also covers any managed service or ongoing consultancy arrangements, and the response and resolution time expectations that govern the support model.
- Related subjects are Knowledge Transfer & Capability (Business & Operations), which covers the customer's internal capability to operate the support model, and Project Governance (Business & Operations), which covers the escalation structures within which support issues are raised and resolved.

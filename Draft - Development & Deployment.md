# Development & Deployment

- Relates to how the solution is developed, tested, and released in a controlled and repeatable way.
- Covers the structure and separation of environments, deployment pipelines, validation gates between environments, rollback procedures, and environment-specific configuration management to ensure changes are tracked and can be reversed where needed.
- Related subjects are Platform Organisation & Capacity (Platform & Security), which covers the workspace structure within which environments are organised, and AI Model Operations (Data & AI), which covers the deployment pipelines specific to AI models in production.

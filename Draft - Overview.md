# Project Areas & Subjects

Areas and subjects define the scope of the framework. An area is a broad domain of the project. Each area is divided into subjects, which represent the specific topics that are assessed, designed, and delivered within that domain.

Every activity within the framework is organised by area and subject. This document defines what each subject covers and how it relates to other subjects across the framework.

Not every subject will be active on every project. Some, such as platform migration or AI governance, are only relevant where those workloads are in scope. Where a subject is not applicable, it is excluded from the project scope and the tasks and activities associated with it do not apply.

# Audit & Compliance

- Relates to the obligations the solution must meet in terms of regulatory requirements, industry standards, or internal policies.
- Covers the audit trail requirements for the solution, the evidence and controls required to demonstrate that the platform operates within defined compliance boundaries, and the process for supporting internal or external compliance reviews.
- Related subjects are Monitoring & Alerting (Platform & Security), which covers the logging mechanisms through which audit trails are captured and retained, and Data Protection (Platform & Security), which covers the data handling requirements that compliance frameworks place on the solution.

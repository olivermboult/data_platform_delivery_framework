# Test Engineer

The Test Engineer is a specialist role assigned to projects where the scope or complexity warrants dedicated testing resource. They contribute consultatively during Discovery and Design to ensure test criteria and success measures are defined before build begins, own the production of the test plan during Design, and lead the technical testing process during Quality Assurance. The role has no involvement on projects where testing complexity does not warrant their assignment.

The Test Engineer role encompasses seniority levels from Test Engineer through to Senior Test Engineer. More senior engineers will typically take ownership of more complex testing workloads and broader areas of the solution, while less experienced engineers may focus on specific components under the oversight of the Data Lead.

**Framework Involvement**

Discovery
- Contributes consultatively to Discovery sessions where test criteria and success measures are being established, ensuring that the agreed success criteria are measurable and testable before Design begins.

Design
- Produces the test plan covering end-to-end testing, performance testing, security testing, and data accuracy validation across all agreed use cases.
- Ensures the test plan is reviewed and agreed by the relevant customer and delivery team stakeholders before Quality Assurance begins.
- Works alongside the Data Consultant, Data Engineer, and specialist roles to ensure that Design decisions are reflected accurately in the test plan.
- Works alongside the Data Engineer and Data Consultant to contribute to the definition of performance targets, ensuring these are captured accurately in the test plan before Quality Assurance begins.

Quality Assurance
- Leads the technical testing process across all areas covered by the agreed test plan, including end-to-end testing, performance testing, security testing, and data accuracy validation.
- Owns the defect logging and triage process, documenting all defects identified during testing and coordinating with the Data Engineer, Reporting Developer, AI/ML Engineer, and I&S Engineer to ensure defects in their respective areas are resolved before the phase closes.
- Manages the UAT process from a coordination perspective, ensuring nominated business users have the scenarios, test cases, and support they need to complete UAT across all agreed use cases.
- Produces the QA completion report summarising the outcomes of all testing activities, the defects identified, and the resolution status of each.
- Submits the QA completion report to the Data Lead for review and recommendation to the Head of Delivery.

Release
- Validates that the production deployment meets the agreed test criteria immediately following release, documenting outcomes and flagging any issues identified during the initial post-release period.

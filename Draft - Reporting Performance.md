# Reporting Performance

- Relates to how reports and dashboards perform under normal and peak usage conditions. 
- Covers report load time expectations, concurrency requirements, the use of caching and pre-aggregation to meet performance targets, and the monitoring of reporting performance once the solution is in production.
- Related subjects are Platform Performance (Platform & Security), which covers the underlying platform performance that reporting performance depends on, Report Audience & Distribution (Reporting & Analysis), which covers the concurrency and peak usage patterns that inform performance targets, and Platform Organisation & Capacity (Platform & Security), which covers the capacity sizing that affects how reports perform under load.

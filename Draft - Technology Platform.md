# Technology Platform

- Relates to the cloud or managed platform on which the solution is built and operated. 
- Covers the identification or confirmation of the chosen platform, any constraints or obligations imposed by existing enterprise agreements or procurement policies, and the configuration and management of the platform throughout delivery. Also covers the compatibility of the chosen platform with data protection, compliance, and architectural requirements identified during the project.
- Related subjects are Data Architecture (Data & AI), which covers how the chosen platform shapes the structural design of the solution, and AI Readiness (Data & AI), which covers the platform capability required to support AI workloads.

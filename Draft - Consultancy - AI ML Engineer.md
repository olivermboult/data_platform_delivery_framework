# AI/ML Engineer

The AI/ML Engineer is a specialist role assigned to projects where AI or machine learning workloads are confirmed as in scope. This role contributes specialist input across all AI-related subjects from Pre-Discovery onwards, working alongside the Data Engineer throughout Delivery. The AI/ML Engineer holds responsibility for designing and implementing the MLOps architecture and leads the build of all AI model components during Build. The role has no involvement on projects where AI workloads are not in scope. 

The AI/ML Engineer role encompasses seniority levels from AI/ML Engineer through to Senior AI/ML Engineer. More senior engineers will typically take ownership of more complex modelling workloads and broader areas of the AI solution, while less experienced engineers may focus on specific components under the oversight of the Data Lead.

**Framework Involvement**

Pre-Discovery
- Delivers AI-related sessions during Platform, Data and AI Initiation where delegated by the Data Lead, capturing detail on the customer's AI readiness, governance position, and any known constraints.
- Produces initial outputs from AI sessions for review by the Data Lead.

Discovery
- Leads AI Discovery sessions across all five subjects: AI Governance and Ethics, MLOps and Model Management, AI Tools, AI Model Development, and AI Readiness.
- Produces initial outputs from AI Discovery sessions for review by the Data Lead.
- Contributes specialist AI input to Platform Discovery and Source System Discovery where AI workloads affect platform and data architecture decisions.

Design
- Leads the production of Design outputs across all AI-related subjects, including AI tooling architecture, MLOps architecture, model development approach, and AI governance framework design.
- Works alongside the Data Consultant and Data Engineer to ensure AI design decisions are consistent with the broader Design.
- Contributes to the design of deployment pipelines and environment strategy where AI model pipelines intersect with the broader infrastructure design.

Platform
- Advises on AI development environment configuration and tooling setup during the Platform phase, ensuring the environment is ready to support AI model development before the Build phase begins.

Build
- Leads the build of all AI model components confirmed in the agreed Design, including feature engineering, model training, validation, and evaluation against the agreed performance criteria.
- Implements the MLOps architecture, covering model versioning, deployment pipelines, monitoring, and retraining processes.
- Works alongside the Data Engineer where AI development and data engineering workloads intersect.
- Conducts unit testing and peer review of all AI components throughout, documenting outcomes and tracking any issues identified for resolution before the phase closes.

Quality Assurance
- Resolves defects identified in AI model outputs and pipelines during testing, documenting outcomes and confirming resolution before the phase closes.

Release
- Validates that AI model outputs meet expectations in the production environment immediately following release and resolves any issues identified during the initial post-release period.

# Project Governance 

- Relates to the formal structures that govern how the project is run, including the people and organisational arrangements that hold authority over it.
- Covers who has decision-making authority, who commissioned the project, and who will be accountable for the solution once it is live. Also covers how progress is tracked and reported, how risks and issues are managed, how changes to scope are assessed and approved, and how decisions are escalated when they cannot be resolved within the delivery team.
- Related subjects are Finance (Business & Operations), which covers the budget framework within which governance decisions are made.

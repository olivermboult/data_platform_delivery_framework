# Project Sponsor

The Project Sponsor is a senior representative on the customer side with authority over budget, scope, and strategic priorities. They are the primary decision-maker throughout the engagement and the main point of contact between the customer organisation and the Data Lead. The Project Sponsor is not expected to be involved in the day-to-day detail of the project but must be available at key decision points throughout each stage to review outputs, provide direction, and grant formal sign-off before the engagement progresses.

**Framework Involvement**

Pre-Discovery
- Acts as the primary source of information on business context, strategic priorities, budget availability, and organisational constraints during Pre-Discovery sessions.
- Confirms the availability and engagement of key stakeholders and subject matter experts for the duration of the project.
- Grants formal sign-off on the recommended path forward at the close of Pre-Discovery before the engagement progresses to Discovery.

Discovery
- Confirms strategic alignment of identified use cases and provides direction on prioritisation where conflicts or trade-offs arise.
- Acts as the primary source of information on budget approval, procurement constraints, and financial boundaries throughout Discovery.
- Reviews and formally signs off the prioritised use case list before the engagement progresses to Design.
- Reviews and formally approves all Discovery outputs, including the discovery report, before the engagement progresses to Design.

Design
- Reviews and formally signs off all Design outputs, including the Design document, source to target mappings, data lineage documentation, reporting wireframes, and operating model design.
- Confirms approval to proceed to the Delivery stage before any build work begins.

Delivery
- Remains available to the Data Lead throughout the Delivery stage for decisions that require customer authority, including scope changes, commercial queries, and escalations.

Quality Assurance
- Reviews the QA completion report produced by the Test Engineer and Data Lead.
- Grants formal sign-off confirming the solution is ready for release before the Release phase begins.

Release
- Confirms that the solution is live and accessible to all agreed user groups following deployment.
- Grants formal sign-off confirming the solution is ready to pass to the support function at the close of Release.

Live
- Participates in the post-project review, providing input on whether the solution has delivered the outcomes it was commissioned to achieve.
- Confirms intent to proceed where a next phase of delivery has been identified.

# Data Protection

- Relates to the handling of sensitive or regulated data within the solution.
- Covers data classification, data masking and anonymisation, data residency and cross-border transfer restrictions, data retention and deletion requirements, and the storage, processing, viewing, and distribution restrictions that protect data throughout its lifecycle. Also covers data loss prevention policies and the processes for managing a data breach or incident where the solution is involved.
- Related subjects are Roles & Access (Platform & Security), which covers how data sensitivity classifications are enforced through platform permissions, and Audit & Compliance (Platform & Security), which covers the regulatory and compliance frameworks that govern how data must be protected.

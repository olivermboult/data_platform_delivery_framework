# Strategic Objectives & Use Cases

- Relates to the specific business problems the project is being commissioned to solve.
- Covers the definition and prioritisation of use cases, the pain points each use case addresses, the expected business benefits, and the success criteria against which delivery will be evaluated. Also covers the alignment of use cases to broader business objectives, any regulatory or contractual drivers that affect prioritisation, and the confirmation that use cases remain within the agreed budget and timeline before design begins.
- Related subjects are Data Modelling (Data & AI), which translates confirmed use cases into data model design, and Report Design (Reporting & Analysis), which translates confirmed use cases into reporting outputs.
